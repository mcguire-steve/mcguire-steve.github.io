I am a Graduate Research Assistant in the Ann and H.J. Smead Department of Aerospace Engineering Sciences, within the College of Engineering and Applied Sciences at the University of Colorado at Boulder under the supervision of Dr. Nisar Ahmed. I am currently a member of COHRINT, the Cooperative Human Interaction Lab and the Boulder Advanced Robotics Lab. I am currently funded through a NASA Space Technology Research Fellowship, grant NNX15AQ14H.

My research interests include human-robot interaction, specifically in techniques to allow a robot to learn about the humans working with it to make informed choices when requesting help from a human. [CV]
